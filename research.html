CURRENT RESEARCH


MODELING

Migration and evolutionary adaptation are expected to be major factors in determining whether species avoid range contraction or outright extinction due to climate change. I use SORTIE, an individual-based forest simulator, to model Sierra Nevada forest dynamics over time under various climate and evolvability scenarios.


HERITABILITY

Analyzing heritable variation in climate response traits can help determine whether real-life tree populations have sufficient genetic diversity to evolve and adapt to future climates.
